Welcome to an Airpod Generation Overview

AirPods are Apple’s revolutionary wireless earbuds, first released in 2016. Over the years, Apple has released several versions with new features and improved performance.

This website provides a quick overview of AirPods, their features, and comparisons between models.

Scroll down for a comparison table or explore the “Models” page for full details.

Why People Love AirPods

  • Seamless pairing with Apple devices
  • Lightweight, portable, and stylish
  • Great sound quality with wireless convenience
